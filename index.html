Eiffel Tower-Paris,France

The Eiffel Tower was built in 1889 to celebrate the French Revolution’s centennial year during the Exposition Universelle at the Champs de Mars. The purpose was to show the other nations the power and the industrial abilities of France. The tower was constructed by the Seine and its rounded shape was used as the entry to the exhibition. The first digging work started on the 26th January 1887, and the tower was finished in record time - 2 years, 2 months and 5 days
